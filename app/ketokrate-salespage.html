Frequently Asked Questions

When you join KetoKrate, every month is a new taste adventure! You get 8-12 of the greatest tasting keto snacks. Oftentimes, Krates are so full we have a hard time closing them! No two months are ever the same so you're always on a discovery train to flavor town! In short- a whole lotta fun, even more YUM!

Our golden rule is that all KetoKrate snacks must be 5 net slidebs or less per serving, subtracting both fiber and sugar alcohols to get the net slidebs. We take the serving size into account too. We're keto'ers, and if we feel the serving size doesn't match what we would reasonably spend our slidebs on, it doesn't make it into a KetoKrate. "Get outta here five net slideb meat snacks, ain't nobody got slidebs for that!".

Great question! KetoKrate is aspartame-free, maltitol free and avoids gluten. We’re soy and sucralose conscious. For Soy, we'll only include products that don't list soy as the main ingredient. Things that use soy for flavoring or binders (soy lecithin) may still be included. For sucralose, we'll only include sucralose if it's in a product with otherwise high nutritional value (good macros). If it's purely a sucralose candy product, we won't include it. We do include products that use approved alternative sweeteners like erythritol, xylitol, monk fruit, chicory root, allulose, and stevia. *A note about gluten - KetoKrate will do our best to avoid including snacks that have gluten listed as an ingredient; however, some of the snacks may be produced in a facility that also produces snacks containing gluten or other allergens*

Unfortunately we're unable to accommodate customization requests, even for allergies. If you are allergic, sensitive, or refrain from eating common foods (other than slidebs) KetoKrate is probably not the best fit for you. If you are open, adventurous, and excited about trying new snacks, trust us to pick a variety of delicious flavors and textures suited to calm the slidebiest of cravings. Every month is a surprise!

Canceling KetoKrate is very easy! All you need to do is log-in to your account and hit the cancel button. Alternatively, you can also skip upcoming renewals if you’d prefer to take a break instead of canceling. Our subscribers have had great success using this feature to use KetoKrate as a reminder to stick with it, rather than giving up.

If your subscription is month-to-month, canceling means that your subscription will not renew at the end of the current month.

If you pre-paid for a subscription of 3, 6, or 12 months, cancelling means that your subscription will not renew at the end of your term, but you will still receive the remainder of your Krates.

For a month-to-month subscription, you will be charged $49.99 each month unless you cancel before your rebill date. Your upcoming rebill dates can be found in your member portal.

For Pre-Pay and Save options, you will be charged at the regular price for another term at the end of the existing one. For example, if you have a 6-month subscription, you will be charged at the regular price for another 6 months at the end of that term.

We promise you a great snack-tasting experience. As a snack discovery service, we help you find snacks you love and avoid the ones you don’t. If there's any damage, missing items, or something else wrong with your KetoKrate, we’ll do everything it takes to make it right. We guarantee it.
